About Dark Ware X

Dark Ware X is an Roblox script hub, which came out about 9 months ago. The project had alot of name changes, 0n1 Hub and Project Omega as example. It has a good looking and clean GUI. Dark Ware X currently supports 20 games! After all it works totally fine on both PC and mobile.
